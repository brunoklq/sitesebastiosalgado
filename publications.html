PRESTIGIOUS

Commander of the Order of Rio Branco (2004)

Brazil: The Order of Rio Branco is one of Brazil's highest honors, bestowed upon individuals who have made significant contributions to diplomacy, international relations, and cultural exchange. Salgado was recognized as a Commander for his outstanding achievements in photography and his efforts to raise awareness about social and environmental issues through his work.

Knight of the Legion of Honour (2016)

France: The Legion of Honour is the highest French order of merit, established by Napoleon Bonaparte in 1802. Salgado was appointed as a Knight in recognition of his exceptional talent as a photographer and his dedication to using his art to promote social justice and environmental conservation

Commander of the Ordre des Arts et des Lettres (2014)

France: The Ordre des Arts et des Lettres (Order of Arts and Letters) is a French order of merit established to recognize individuals who have contributed significantly to the arts and literature. Salgado was honored as a Commander for his remarkable body of work documenting human life, environmental issues, and social injustice.

Knight of the Order of Cultural Merit (2018)

Monaco: The Order of Cultural Merit is one of the highest honors bestowed by the Principality of Monaco, recognizing individuals who have made outstanding contributions to culture and the arts. Salgado was named a Knight in acknowledgment of his profound impact on the world of photography and his advocacy for social and environmental causes.

These honors reflect the widespread recognition and appreciation for Sebastião Salgado's talent, passion, and dedication to using photography as a tool for social change and awareness. Through his powerful imagery and advocacy work, Salgado continues to inspire audiences around the world and make a meaningful difference in addressing some of the most pressing issues of our time.

Sebastião Salgado has been recognized with several prestigious honors for his contributions to photography and his advocacy for social and environmental causes.
